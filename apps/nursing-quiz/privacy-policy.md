## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **NursingQuiz**. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Data We Collect

Our app does not require a user login or account creation. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers.

### Locally Stored Data

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

- **Quiz data** is stored in a locally encrypted SQLite database using SQLCipher with AES-256 encryption. This includes quiz questions, your answers, scores, and progress. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.
- **User preferences** such as theme settings and app configuration are stored locally using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device.

No quiz history, scores, or user data is backed up to the cloud or sent to any server.

### Analytics Data

The app uses **Google Analytics for Firebase** to collect anonymous usage data such as session duration, screens visited, and general app interaction patterns. This helps us understand how the app is used and improve its functionality. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

### Advertising Data

The app uses **Google AdMob** to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use device identifiers and usage data to serve and personalize ads.

## 3. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates services provided by Google. These services process data according to their own privacy policies:

- [Google Play Services](https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/)
- [Google Analytics for Firebase](https://firebase.google.com/policies/analytics)
- [AdMob](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543?hl=en)

On iOS, the app does not track you across other companies' apps and websites (NSPrivacyTracking is disabled).

## 4. Data Retention and Security

- **Quiz data** is stored locally in an encrypted database (SQLCipher AES-256) and is never transmitted. It persists until you uninstall the app or clear app data.
- **User preferences** are stored locally and persist until you uninstall the app or clear app data.
- **Analytics data** is managed by Google's secure infrastructure and follows their retention policies.
- **Ad-related data** is managed by Google's secure infrastructure and follows their retention policies.

We do not operate any servers that store your data.

## 5. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take necessary actions.

## 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective when posted on this page.
