## NursingQuiz

A secure, offline-first mobile application for nursing professionals and students preparing for competitive nursing exams like NORCET, AIIMS, DSSSB, PGI, ESIC, RPSC, RUHS, and more. Available on Android and iOS. Provides a comprehensive quiz experience with encrypted local storage, performance statistics, short notes flashcards, and dark mode -- all without requiring an internet connection or user account.
