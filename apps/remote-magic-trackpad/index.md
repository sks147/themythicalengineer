## Tips

- **Disable 3-finger swipe screenshot shortcut** on your Android phone. Many Android devices use a 3-finger swipe gesture to take screenshots, which intercepts the gesture before it reaches the app. To use all Mac trackpad gestures (e.g., three-finger drag, Mission Control, App Exposé), go to your phone's **Settings → Gestures** (or **Advanced Features**) and turn off the screenshot swipe shortcut.
