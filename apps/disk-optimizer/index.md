## Disk Optimizer

Disk Optimizer helps you analyze and reclaim storage space on your Android device. Scan your device to visualize disk usage, quickly clean up large files, and compress photos and videos to free up space.

### Features

- Visual storage breakdown by category (images, videos, audio, documents, APKs)
- Drill-down file explorer sorted by size
- Quick cleanup with swipe-based file review
- Batch photo and video compression with quality presets
- In-app trash with restore capability
- Installed apps viewer with sizes and last-used timestamps
