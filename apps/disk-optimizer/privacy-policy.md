## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **Disk Optimizer**. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Data We Collect

Our app does not require a user login or account creation. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers.

### Device Storage Data

Disk Optimizer scans your device's filesystem to build a map of files, directories, and their sizes. This data exists only in the device's memory while the app is running and is never stored permanently or transmitted to any server.

### Installed App Information

The app queries your device for installed app sizes (app, data, and cache) and last-used timestamps to display in the installed apps viewer. This information is used locally for display purposes only and is never transmitted.

### Locally Stored Data

The app stores a small number of preferences locally on your device using SharedPreferences:

- Theme mode (System, Light, or Dark)
- Display size scaling
- Onboarding completion flag

Files you move to the in-app trash are stored in the app's private storage directory on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not backed up to the cloud.

### Analytics Data

The app uses **Google Analytics for Firebase** to collect anonymous usage data such as screens visited and optimization events (number of files processed, bytes saved). This helps us understand how the app is used and improve its functionality. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

### Crash Reporting Data

The app uses **Firebase Crashlytics** to automatically collect crash reports and diagnostic data when the app encounters an error. This may include device model, OS version, app state at the time of the crash, and stack traces. This data is used solely to identify and fix bugs. No personally identifiable information is collected through crash reporting.

### Advertising Data

The app uses **Google AdMob** to display banner advertisements. AdMob may collect and use device identifiers and usage data to serve and personalize ads.

## 3. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates services provided by Google. These services process data according to their own privacy policies:

- [Google Play Services](https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/)
- [Google Analytics for Firebase](https://firebase.google.com/policies/analytics)
- [Firebase Crashlytics](https://firebase.google.com/terms/crashlytics)
- [AdMob](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543?hl=en)

## 4. Data Retention and Security

- **Storage scan data** is held in memory only while the app is in use and is not persisted or transmitted.
- **User preferences** are stored locally on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data.
- **Trash files** remain in the app's private storage until you restore or permanently delete them, or until you uninstall the app.
- **Analytics data** is managed by Google's secure infrastructure and follows their retention policies.
- **Crash reporting data** is managed by Google's secure infrastructure and follows their retention policies.
- **Ad-related data** is managed by Google's secure infrastructure and follows their retention policies.

We do not operate any servers that store your data.

## 5. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take necessary actions.
